Buyer questions

Clarity before you commit budget, time, and internal momentum.

Straight answers for teams evaluating a SaaS-grade LMS rollout, migration, or analytics improvement project.

Can you help us choose the right LMS?

Yes. Discovery can compare platforms by audience type, admin complexity, integrations, analytics needs, and long-term operating cost.

Do you only configure the platform?

No. Platform delivery works best when audience structure, reporting logic, governance, enablement, and adoption planning are handled together.

Can legacy content and learner history move cleanly?

Yes. The method depends on the source platform, content standards, and data quality, but migration planning is a core part of the work.

Can AI be added without rebuilding everything?

Often yes. AI can be introduced through workflow improvements, assistant-led content production, smarter reporting, and selective automation without a full rebuild.
